How has computing got here today?

The first programmable computer, the Z1, was created between 1936 and 1938 by Konrad Zuse in his parents' living room    Read More →

Since then, computers have gotten smaller and faster to the present day where computers are all around us. Instead of having a room dedicated to one computer, all you need now is your pocket.

All this has been enabled to evolving technology, such as 7 nanometer long transistors. This allows billions of transistors to fit on a small chip in the latest smartphones.
